publication: '*Simulation of Semiconductor Devices and Processes*'
abstract: We present a new algorithm for the generation of 3-dimensional (3-D) grids
  for the simulation of semiconductor devices. The fitting of the device geometry
  and the required mesh density is obtained by partitioning the elements at an optimal
  point at each refinement step. This allows the fitting of more general 3-D device
  geometries and the reduction of grid points in comparison with previous grid generators.
